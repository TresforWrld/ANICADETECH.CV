Terms of Use

This tool helps users create CV documents from the information they provide. Users are responsible for ensuring that all content is truthful, accurate and appropriate for job applications.

The generator is provided for convenience and does not guarantee employment, interview selection or recruiter approval. Always review the generated CV before sending it to employers.

Privacy Policy

All CV information is processed entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. The Recent CVs feature uses your browser's localStorage — data stays on your device and is never uploaded.

Do not enter sensitive information not needed for a job application, such as passwords, bank details or national identity numbers.

AI Assistant

The AI Assistant uses the Anthropic Claude API to help polish your CV text. Your prompts and CV content snippets are sent to the API for this purpose. Do not include passwords or highly sensitive personal data in AI-enhanced fields.
