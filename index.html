Privacy Policy

All CV information is processed entirely in your browser. The Recent CVs and auto-save draft features use your browser's localStorage — data stays on your device and is never uploaded to ANICADE Tech servers.

Do not enter sensitive information not needed for a job application, such as passwords, bank details or national identity numbers.

AI Assistant

The AI Assistant and AI Enhance features use free, publicly available text-generation and language-checking services to help polish your CV text, plus a built-in offline knowledge base of common CV questions. Do not include passwords or highly sensitive personal data in AI-enhanced fields, since some requests are sent to third-party services over the network.
